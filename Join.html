How to join Real Miles Trucking

Requirements to join

  • Respect everyone, no discrimination or any kind of harrassment.
  • In the voice channels be also respectful to each other.
  • English is the main language of this server.
  • Keep swearing to a minimum.
  • At least 17 years old.
  • Apply on TMP and Trucksbook.
  • Use the in-game tag and paint correctly.
  • Always follow the rules set by truckersMP.
  • Do not spam, advertising can only after consulting staff.
  • Being inactive without notice will result in removal of the VTC.

    • Step 1

  • Log in or register on Discord
  • Join our server by clicking here and accept the server rules.

    • Step 2

  • Go to TrucksBook, register using Steam and log in.
  • Apply for the VTC on this page.
  • Go to the main page after logging in and click on download client.
  • Or download on our website here.
  • Install and log in.

    • Step 3

  • Go to TruckersMP, register using Steam and log in.
  • Apply for the VTC on this page.
